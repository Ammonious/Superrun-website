How it Works

All students participate in the Super Run Course and run as many laps as they can for at least 20 mins.


You can make a pledge per minute or a flat donation. Pledges per minute will be calculated by multiplying the entered amount by 20 minutes. For example a $1.00 Pledge will be a total donation of $20.00.
