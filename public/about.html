About Us

Super Run is a fundraiser for elementary schools who want to earn more profits, get more student involvement, and have more fun!

SUPER RUN

Super Run is designed to bring everyone together on the same team.

Our goal is to help earn more profit for your school, give students a chance to learn qualities that are character-building and will help them be better citizens, and have fun while participating in a healthy, exciting activity that will be fun, engaging and memorable.
