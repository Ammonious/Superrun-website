What is Super Run?

SUPER RUN is a fundraiser for elementary schools who want to EARN MORE PROFITS, GET MORE STUDENT INVOLVEMENT, AND HAVE MORE FUN.

SUPER RUN is designed to bring everyone together on the same team (administration, faculty, PTA, parents and students) to help earn more profit for the school, give the students a chance to learn qualities that are character-building and will help them be better citizens, and have fun while participating in a healthy, exciting activity that will be fun, engaging and memorable. We are a local Utah Company and have vested interest in our local school.
