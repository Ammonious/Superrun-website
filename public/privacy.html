SUPER RUN PRIVACY POLICY

We at Super Run value the trust our super run participants place in our company. We take reasonable steps to safeguard that trust by implementing measures aimed at keeping nonpublic personal information about super run participants in a secure environment, and by using that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We gather only the information that we need to administer our business, our security and the security of our super run participants in order to provide superior products and service.

SUPER RUN

Visiting Our Website

You can visit the SUPER RUN website (“Website”) without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. We collect and monitor user traffic patterns throughout the Website according to non-personally identifiable information such as a user’s domain name, IP address, browser type, date and time of access and pages viewed. This non-personally identifiable information is collected in order to measure the number of visitors to our Website and to determine which areas of the Website receive the most traffic.

SUPER RUN

Information We Collect and How We Use It

How We Collect Information. Most information comes directly from the Parents/Guardians of fun run participants when they access and use our Internet services (“Services”). When we use the term “Business Information” in this privacy policy, we mean information that tells us specifically who a visitor is and their contact information, including their name, email address, street address and telephone number. We collect Business Information when a guardian/representative of a super run participant uses our Services or provides information at our events. Visitors to our Website may also provide Business Information and other data by pledging money towards a super run participant.
